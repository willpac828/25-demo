Nice to Meet You!

Hey, my name is Will. You're reading my About Me section, presumably of your own volition. While I have your attention, I'd like to tell you a bit about myself. I was born in ATL, but I've called Nashville home since 1999. I consider myself a lifelong learner; for most of my life I've enjoyed messing around with various spoken languages, and at some point I became aware of computer languages.

Until recently, the South has never seemed like much of a hub for "tech". At the very least, it wasn't the same, giant entity that it seemed to be in other parts of the country. Kids grew up wanting to be teachers and doctors and lawyers and engineers. The tech industry seemed like a distant, esoteric idea better left to west-coast compatriots. Accordingly, I took the internet completely for granted for most of my life. I gradually became conscious of the fact that making internet content consisted of countless moving parts, however, and I found myself drawn to learning those technologies for myself. Nowadays, I'm proud to call myself a web developer, and I don't plan on switching gears anytime soon.

When I'm not reading up on cool CSS transitions or listening to JavaScript podcasts, you can probably find me flipping through French or Hungarian flashcards, playing with the most recent Magic the Gathering set, finding the coolest patio bars in Nashville, or figuring out how I'm going to make it to the Tokyo Olympics in 2020.
